About Me

Hi, my name is Mia Garcia, an analyst at an IT consulting company. I graduated from the University of Maryland, College Park, and currently a student at Drexel University to pursue my Master's in Library and Information Science.

I am from El Paso, Texas but moved to Pennsylvania for work. I was very nervous to move to a city I had never visited, a place where I didn't have any friends or family! It was a very exciting time and three years later, I have the bestest friends and found a great community here.

I am also a short story writer, attempting to finish my novella. I self published in college, but hoping that one day I'll make my big break!

As a writer, I have a huge love of books. I read often and even run an Instagram account of books I've read and provide reviews. I've been recently busy with work and school that I haven't had a chance to post but please check it out!

I also have two dogs and a cat! They can be a handful sometimes but they are too cute to be upset at!

I like to do a lot of physical activities since I work from a desk all day. These include going to run club, my kickball games, and regular excercise. I will admit, I do get lazy sometimes and will graciously give myself a rest day when I feel like it!

I've recently considered joining a pickleball league, but my schedule is already pretty busy! I'll let you know if it happens!
