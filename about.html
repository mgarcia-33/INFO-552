Mission Statement

Our mission is to create a dedicated reference hub for individuals with disabilities. Our platform intentionally serves as a safe, inclusive environment where members can connect, share experiences, and stay informed about news, events, and opportunities that matter to them. Inspired by bold, unfiltered self-expression that's found in Charli XCX’s BRAT, we’re committed to fostering a space where authenticity is celebrated and community thrives. Through accessible, community-centered resources, we aim to cultivate belonging and empowerment because we are better together.

Purpose Statement

This site was created for people with disabilities to have a space that truly feels like theirs. It’s a place to find helpful information, stay informed about news and events, and connect with others in the community. Unlike general sites made for allies, this one is just for you. This is a supportive, welcoming space where you can share, grow, and celebrate what makes this community strong.
